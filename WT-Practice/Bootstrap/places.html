Places to Visit in Kolhapur

Mahalaxmi Temple

One of the Shakti Peethas dedicated to Goddess Mahalaxmi, this temple is over 1000 years old and a major pilgrimage site.

Rankala Lake

A scenic lake with a historical Shiva temple in the middle. Perfect for evening walks and boating.

New Palace

The residence of the erstwhile Maharajas of Kolhapur, now a museum showcasing royal artifacts and a zoo.
