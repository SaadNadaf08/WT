About Kolhapur

Kolhapur is a historic city in Maharashtra, India, known for its rich cultural heritage, magnificent temples, and delicious cuisine. Situated on the banks of the Panchganga River, Kolhapur is often referred to as 'Dakshin Kashi' due to its spiritual significance.

The city is famous for its Kolhapuri chappals, jewelry, and especially the Kolhapuri cuisine which is known for its spicy flavors. The Mahalaxmi Temple is one of the most important pilgrimage sites in the region.
